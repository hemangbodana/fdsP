Favourite Design Studio was established in 2010 by Mr. Vipul Mistry, who serves as the Founder and Director of the firm. With a professional legacy spanning over 15 years, the firm has evolved into a multidisciplinary practice offering comprehensive services in Architecture, Interior Design, and Vastu Consultancy across India.

At Favourite Design Studio, we are committed to delivering design solutions that balance innovation, functionality, and contextual relevance. Each project is approached with a deep understanding of client needs, site dynamics, and cultural context, ensuring outcomes that are both timeless and distinctive.

Our portfolio reflects a diverse range of projects — from residential and commercial developments to Institutional and Healthcare spaces — all unified by a commitment to quality, precision, and design excellence.

Guided by a clear design vision and a client-centric philosophy, Favourite Design Studio continues to shape spaces that inspire, engage, and endure.
